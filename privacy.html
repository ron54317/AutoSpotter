1. Introduction

AutoSpotterX ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the AutoSpotterX mobile app (available on Google Play). It applies to all users of the app and covers the data collected through the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account & Profile Data: Email address, password (stored securely), username, first name, last name, optional profile photo, account creation date, and AutoXP points.

User Content: Vehicle photos (low-quality thumbnails stored in Firebase and high-quality photos stored in Cloudinary for feed display), car make/model information (entered manually or via AI detection), AI detection confidence scores, notes/captions, timestamps, optional GPS coordinates (stored only on your device‚Äîsee Section 6.1), country name/code (derived from coordinates), likes (your user ID when you like posts), privacy settings, friend connections and requests (including friend request status, timestamps, and email addresses for pending invitations), notifications (including like notifications with liker information and car details, friend request notifications with requester information, and system notifications), and reports (when you report content).

Device & Usage Data: Technical data from your device, such as device model and operating system version, to ensure compatibility and diagnose issues. We may also collect usage statistics to improve the app.

App Permissions: Camera (to take photos), Storage (to save photos and data), Location (for mapping features), Internet (for cloud sync), and Network State (to check connectivity). You can change or revoke permissions through your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services and Partners

AutoSpotterX uses third-party services (each has its own privacy policy):

  • Firebase (Google): User accounts, database storage, and backend services. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google ML Kit: On-device license plate and face blurring. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini API: Optional AI car identification. Only the photo is sent (not stored by Google). Limited free uses per account. (Terms)
  • Google Geocoding Service: Converts GPS coordinates to country name/code. Coordinates are sent to Google for lookup (may be collected per Google's Privacy Policy). Only country info is stored in our database. (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudinary: High-quality photo storage and delivery for the public feed. We store high-quality versions of your car photos on Cloudinary (in addition to low-quality thumbnails in Firebase) to provide better image quality in the feed. High-quality photos may be periodically deleted from Cloudinary for storage management, but low-quality thumbnails remain in Firebase. (Privacy Policy)
  • MapTiler: Map tiles (no access to your location data). (Privacy Policy)
  • MapLibre GL: Map rendering (runs entirely on your device).

  • Gamification: Calculate AutoXP points to unlock features, display XP ranks (Newbie, Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert, Master, Legend), and power leaderboard rankings (top 50 users by AutoXP, showing username, profile photo, and XP).
  • AI Services: Provide car identification via Google Gemini (usage tracked for limits).
  • Privacy Controls: Respect your feed visibility preferences.
  • Mapping: Display car locations on your personal map (coordinates stored locally‚Äîsee Section 6.1).
  • App Improvement: Analyze anonymous vehicle data to improve car specifications database.

  • Authentication: Secure password authentication (passwords never stored in plain text).
  • Local Processing: Car detection, blurring, and watermarking done on your device. Original images stay on your device.
