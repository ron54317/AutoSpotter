1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CarSpotter ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and secure manner.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. App Permissions:

  • Camera: Required to take photos of vehicles. We only access the camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo within the app.
  • Storage: Required to save vehicle photos to your device and manage your car collection. We only access files related to our app's functionality.

2.2. Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)

2.3. User Content:

  • Photos of vehicles you upload
  • Vehicle information you provide
  • Your personal car collection data

2.4. Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers

2.5. Usage Data:

  • How you interact with the app
  • Features you use
  • Time spent in the app
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

3.1. Firebase Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user account management
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: To securely store your vehicle photos
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To identify and fix app crashes
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience

These Firebase services may collect device information and usage data. You can learn more about Firebase's data practices in their Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your car spotting photos
  • Maintain your personal car collection
  • Improve the app's functionality
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Send important notifications about the app
  • Ensure the security of our services

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. Data Storage:

  • Your data is stored on secure servers
  • Photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • We regularly backup data to prevent loss

5.2. Security Measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure user authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our legal rights
  • With service providers who help operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • In the event of a business transfer or merger

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law

After account deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying you through the app
