1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CarSpotter ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in a transparent and secure manner.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. App Permissions:

  • Internet: Required to connect to our servers, Firebase services, and retrieve car information.
  • Camera: Required to take photos of vehicles. We only access the camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo within the app.
  • Storage/Media: Required to save vehicle photos to your device and manage your car collection. We only access files related to our app's functionality.
  • Network State: Required to determine if you are connected to the internet and adjust app functionality accordingly.
  • Location: Required to provide location-based features, including mapping spotted cars and showing nearby car spots. We access your precise location only when you explicitly use map-related features.

2.2. Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • First and last name
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile photo (optional)

2.3. User Content:

  • Photos of vehicles you upload
  • Vehicle information you provide
  • Your personal car collection data
  • Car specifications that you provide for vehicles not in our database
  • Friend relationships and shared content
  • Location data associated with car spottings (when location features are enabled)
  • Images shared from other apps to CarSpotter

2.4. Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (non-personal)

2.5. Usage Data:

  • How you interact with the app
  • Features you use
  • Time spent in the app
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • AI detection usage metrics
  • Map usage and interaction patterns

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

3.1. Firebase Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user account management
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: To securely store your vehicle photos
  • Firebase Firestore: To store and retrieve car data and user preferences
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: For server-side processing of certain features

3.2. AI Technologies:

  • Google ML Kit: For text recognition to detect license plates, and for face detection to protect privacy
  • TensorFlow Lite: For on-device car detection (no images are sent to external servers for this processing)

3.3. Mapping Services:

  • MapLibre GL: For displaying maps and car spotting locations within the app (open-source mapping library that processes data locally)

These services may collect device information and usage data. You can learn more about their data practices in their respective privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • MapLibre Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your car spotting photos
  • Maintain your personal car collection
  • Improve the app's functionality
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Send important notifications about the app
  • Ensure the security of our services
  • Detect cars in your photos using on-device AI
  • Automatically blur license plates and faces in photos to protect privacy
  • Enable social features like friend connections
  • Facilitate sharing of car collection data between friends
  • Provide location-based features such as mapping spotted cars
  • Process images shared from other apps
  • Generate location-based car spotting statistics
  • Add car specifications to our database when you provide information for vehicles not already in our system

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. Data Storage:

  • Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers
  • Photos are stored in encrypted Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text, only securely hashed
  • We use Firebase's automatic backup systems to prevent data loss
  • Car detection models run locally on your device, not on our servers
  • Location data is securely stored with restricted access
  • License plate and face detection/blurring occurs entirely on your device

5.2. Security Measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS
  • Secure Firebase user authentication
  • Regular security updates to the app
  • ProGuard code obfuscation to prevent reverse engineering
  • API keys are securely stored and not embedded in app code
  • Firebase security rules to prevent unauthorized data access
  • Friend request verification to protect user privacy
  • Secure handling of shared content from other apps
  • Access controls for location data
  • Automatic blurring of license plates and faces in vehicle photos

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights or users' safety
  • With service providers like Firebase who help operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • With friends you have explicitly connected with in the app (limited to car collection data you choose to share)
  • In the event of a business transfer or merger (you would be notified)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app's profile settings
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete your account and associated data through the app settings
  • Export your car collection data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Manage friend connections and shared content
  • Disable location sharing in your device settings or app preferences
  • Control which images are shared with the app from other applications
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law

When you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days. We may retain anonymized data for analytics purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

10. AI Features and Privacy

10.1. Car Detection:

  • Our app uses TensorFlow Lite for on-device car detection
  • This technology helps verify if photos contain vehicles
  • All car detection processing happens locally on your device
  • Free accounts can access car detection for all photos
  • No images are sent to external servers for car detection

10.2. AI Accuracy Disclaimer:

  • Our car detection system makes best efforts to identify vehicles correctly but is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate
  • Users should verify all AI-generated information before relying on it
  • The AI system does not learn from or adapt based on user data
  • AI processing uses on-device (TensorFlow Lite) technology

10.3. Privacy Protection in Images:

  • The app automatically detects and blurs license plates in all vehicle photos using Google ML Kit
  • The app automatically detects and blurs faces that may appear in vehicle photos using Google ML Kit
  • All license plate and face detection/blurring occurs entirely on your device
  • No unblurred images containing license plates or faces are stored or transmitted
  • These privacy protections apply to both photos taken within the app and images shared from other applications
  • While we make best efforts to detect and blur all sensitive information, the system is not guaranteed to be 100% effective, and users should verify photos before sharing

11. Social Features and Friend Connections

Our app includes social features that allow you to:

  • Send and receive friend requests
  • View friends' car collections
  • Share your car collection with friends
  • Search for friends within the app

When you connect with another user as a friend, they will be able to see all cars in your collection. Your email and profile photo will be visible to users you connect with. Friend connections are stored in our Firebase Firestore database and are only visible to the connected users.

12. Location Data and Mapping

When you enable location features:

  • We collect your precise location only when you use map-related features
  • Location data may be associated with car spottings you upload
  • Map data is processed using MapLibre GL, an open-source mapping library
  • All map rendering happens locally on your device
  • No third-party map providers receive your personal information
  • You can disable location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Location history is stored securely and accessible only to you and those you explicitly share with
  • The map displays car spots from your collection and those shared by your friends
  • You can filter map displays to show only your cars or recent spots
  • Important: When you add location data to a car photo, that photo will be visible to all users on the public map. This means anyone using the app can see your car spotting at that location. Please be mindful of this when sharing location data.
  • You can choose to share car photos without location data to maintain privacy
  • Location data is only shared when you explicitly enable it for a specific car spotting

13. Image Sharing Features

Our app can receive images shared from other applications:

  • When you share images from your gallery or other apps to CarSpotter, we process these images to add them to your collection
  • Shared images are processed for car detection and privacy protection (license plate and face blurring)
  • Shared images are stored securely using the same protections as photos taken within the app
  • Metadata from shared images (such as location or timestamps) may be processed if available
  • You maintain control over images shared to the app and can delete them at any time

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the app
