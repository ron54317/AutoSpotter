2. Description of Service

AutoSpotter is a specialized mobile application designed for car enthusiasts and spotters. The service includes the following key features:

  • Car spotting and photo sharing capabilities
  • Car information database access
  • Personal car collection management
  • Car rarity classification and tracking
  • Social features including friends and car collection sharing
  • Profile customization
  • AI-powered car detection (local and cloud-based)
  • Intelligent car identification using Google Gemini AI (optional, with usage limits)

  • Image sharing integration and automatic processing
  • Advanced filtering and validation systems
  • Public spot feed with community interaction (likes, reports)
